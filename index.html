About Me

Hi guys, I am a UI/UX designer with a good user research background. I have helped teams in college by improving and redesigning applications.

I specialize in product development, UI design, UX design, interactive design. I have done projects in internet of things, front end development. I have also worked in front end development to design a online application, I have used HTML5, CSS3, Bootstrap, PHP, JQuery, javaScript frameworks in these projects. I have worked on Internet of things to create ambient displays, artifacts front end designs to display live APIs.

I BELIEVE IN MAKING IDEAS GREAT

The most important part in product design is user experience because it allows higher productivity, it provides potential customers with a satisfed browsing experience. User experience helps to make products very simple and fast.

UI is also one of the important aspects in desgning, "We do not admit it but people usually judge the book by its cover." I feel key to good UI is to have a consistent theme from screen to screen.
